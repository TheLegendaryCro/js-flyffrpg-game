Play this example RPG arena game!

Choose your character!

Templar

Templars have higher health and stamina, however their strength also makes them slower and clumsier.

Rogues

Rogues are fast and have high stamina and speed which make up for their lack of strength and health.

Mage

Mages are glass canons which put all their effort into spells. This means they slack on everything else.

Ranger

Rangers are well rounded fighters that focus on evening out their skills. No strengths or weaknesses.
